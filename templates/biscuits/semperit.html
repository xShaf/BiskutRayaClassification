Biscuit: Semperit

Recipe

  • 200g corn flour (tepung jagung)
  • 120g wheat flour
  • 180g butter
  • 100g icing sugar
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence
  • Yellow food colouring
  • Red cherries (cut small) or colored sprinkles for topping

Instructions

  1. Cream butter and icing sugar until fluffy. Add egg yolk and vanilla. Mix well.
  2. Add corn flour and wheat flour gradually. Mix until dough is soft but not sticky.
  3. Add yellow food colouring if desired. Mix evenly.
  4. Use a semperit flower nozzle with piping bag. Pipe onto baking tray lined with baking paper.
  5. Decorate each cookie with a small piece of cherry or sprinkles.
