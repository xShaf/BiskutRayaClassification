Biscuit: Bahulu

Recipe

  • 5 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla essence
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour (wheat flour)

Instructions

  1. Add sugar and eggs, then beat until well combined. Add vanilla essence and continue beating for 5 minutes.
    (Beat until the mixture becomes fluffy and changes color for soft bahulu)
  2. Add the flour and beat for another 2 minutes.
  3. Preheat the oven to 150¬∞C for 10 minutes with the mold inside.
  4. Remove the mold and grease it once with oil to prevent sticking.
  5. Pour the batter into the mold, filling about 3/4 full. Bake at 150¬∞C for 12 minutes (or adjust based on your oven).

History

According to traditional tales, the word "BAHULU" or "BAULU" is believed to have originated from "buah tangan dari hulu" (a gift from upstream), referring to a time when rivers were the main transport route. Bahulu was given as a gift to symbolize kindness and strengthen relationships among neighbors and family.
