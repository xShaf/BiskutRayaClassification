Biscuit: Almond London

Recipe

  • 250g butter
  • 90g icing sugar
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 300g plain flour
  • 100g corn flour
  • Roasted almonds (whole)
  • Cooking chocolate for coating
  • Chocolate rice or chopped nuts for garnish

Instructions: Mix butter, sugar, egg yolk, and flour. Wrap dough around almonds. Bake at 170Â°C for 20 mins. Dip in melted chocolate and garnish.

History

Almond London cookies are popular festive treats in Malaysia, particularly during Hari Raya. These bite-sized biscuits consist of crunchy almonds wrapped in a buttery dough, coated in chocolate, and topped with sprinkles or nuts. The name likely references the European influence on Malaysian baking styles.
